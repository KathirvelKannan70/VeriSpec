SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS SPECIFICATION (SRS)
Project: Wolters Kluwer Tax & Compliance Portal
Version: 1.0.0
Author: QA Engineering Team

---------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1: Authentication & Access Control

REQ-1: The compliance portal shall enforce secure user authentication.
REQ-2: Upon entering valid credentials, the system must prompt the user for a 6-digit Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) token.
REQ-3: Unauthorized login attempts shall be blocked after 5 failed tries and logged in the security audits.

---------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: Tax Document Ingestion & Parser

REQ-4: The ingestion module shall parse incoming IRS Form 1040 PDF documents.
REQ-5: The parsing engine must automatically validate all financial fields against the IRS rules database.
REQ-6: Any calculated tax discrepancy shall be visually flagged in red on the auditor interface.

---------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: Ledger Audit Trails

REQ-7: The client tax ledger shall record all financial modification actions.
REQ-8: When an auditor modifies a deduction value, the system shall save a ledger log.
REQ-9: Audit logs must log the timestamp, user ID, old value, and updated value for compliance verification.
